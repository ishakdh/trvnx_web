Privacy Policy & B2B Usage Agreement

Effective Date: April 2026 | TRVNX Ecosystem

Google Play Protect Compliance Notice: The application "Lindux Staff Pro" (com.lindux.staffpro) is a proprietary B2B Enterprise Mobility Management (EMM) tool. It is intentionally excluded from the public Google Play Store as it requires Device Owner provisioning via QR code to secure retail hardware assets against EMI default.

1. Purpose of the Application

Lindux Staff Pro is a device security and management application built exclusively for corporate clients and mobile retail showrooms. Its primary function is to secure hardware assets deployed under EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) financing contracts. By establishing Device Owner privileges during factory provisioning, the software ensures the physical asset remains secured until the financial contract is fulfilled.

2. Required Permissions & Device Administrator

To function as an EMI security tool, Lindux Staff Pro strictly requires BIND_DEVICE_ADMIN (Device Owner) privileges. This allows the application to legitimately execute the following asset-protection protocols:

  • Restrict access to the device UI (Lock Screen) in the event of payment default.
  • Disable Safe Mode and Factory Resets to prevent unauthorized circumvention of the EMI lock.
  • Prevent application uninstallation by unauthorized users.

3. Data Collection and Telemetry

Lindux operates under strict data minimization principles. We do NOT collect personal browsing history, SMS messages, user photos, or personal contacts.

The application only collects and transmits the following hardware telemetry to our secure TRVNX servers to maintain the integrity of the lock status:

  • Hardware Identifiers (Android ID) to verify the specific device under contract.
  • Network status to ensure the device remains connected to our management server.
  • Device lock state and heartbeat pings to verify the software has not been tampered with.

4. Data Retention and Asset Release

Once the financing term is completed, the retailer initiates a permanent unlock command from the server. The Lindux Staff Pro application actively removes its own Device Owner privileges and initiates a full self-uninstallation protocol, returning the device to standard, unmanaged consumer functionality. All associated telemetry for that specific hardware ID is permanently purged from our active operational databases.
